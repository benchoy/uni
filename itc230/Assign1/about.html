About Nuts R Us

Nuts R Us are an online wholesale distributer of nuts, bolts, washers, screws, and fasteners. The company is an Australian owned with it's distribution centre located in Sydney, and was established in 2011.

Our staff are experienced are are available to assist with all enquiries and/or order fulfilments to ensure you get exactly the products you need quickly and at a competitive price.

As a distributor we supply directly to both the public and private sector.

Our strength is our knowledge and range, if we don't stock it then it doesn't exist.
